# Case 02: Fake Company Investigation

## Context

A recruiter-style message mentions a company called:

`NovaTrust Digital Services`

The sender claims the company provides payment security consulting and digital trust services. The message links to:

`novatrust-digital-example.com`

This is a simulated case. The company, domain, records, and findings are fictional.

## Business Scenario

The company claims to be a growing digital services provider with international clients. The message asks the recipient to complete an onboarding form and provide identity documents.

The request is sensitive enough that the company identity should be checked before any interaction.

## Investigation Goal

Assess whether the company and domain have a believable public footprint and whether the technical setup supports the claimed business identity.

## Initial Hypothesis

The company may be fake or recently created for fraud. I want to test that hypothesis by checking the domain, digital presence, email setup, and consistency of the public story.

## Rules of Engagement

- Passive OSINT only
- No contact with the company
- No form submission
- No account creation
- No document upload
- No active scanning

## Target Assets

- Company name: `NovaTrust Digital Services`
- Domain: `novatrust-digital-example.com`
- Indicator type: domain / claimed organization
